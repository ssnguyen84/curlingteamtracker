Curling Team Tracker

The purpose of the project is to create an application to track the performance of curling teams and individual players within that team.

Class Team
	Attributes: teamName, currentTeammates, eventsAttendance, moneyEarned, standings, provinceRepresentation.
	
Class Player
	Attributes: firstName, lastName, shootingPerformance, previousTeam, currentTeam, currentProvince, previousProvince, eventHistory, moneyEarnedIndividual
	
Use Cases:
	Calculate money earned by each player.
	Calculate shooting performance over the year.
	Change team player.
